# Bitwise operator_비트 연산자

## & (AND)
| 0 | & | 0 | = | 0 |<br>
| 0 | & | 1 | = | 0 |<br>
| 1 | & | 1 | = | 1 |<br>

## | (OR)
| 0 | | | 0 | = | 0 |<br>
| 0 | | | 1 | = | 1 |<br>
| 1 | | | 1 | = | 1 |<br>

## ^ (XOR)
| 0 | ^ | 0 | = | 0 |<br>
| 0 | ^ | 1 | = | 1 |<br>
| 1 | ^ | 1 | = | 0 |<br>

## ~ (NOT)
~ | 0 | = | 1 |<br>
~ | 1 | = | 0 |<br>

## << (LEFT SHIFT)
| 0 | 0 | 0 | 1 | << 2 = | 0 | 1 | 0 | 0 |

## >> (RIGHT SHIFT)
| 0 | 1 | 0 | 0 | >> 2 = | 0 | 0 | 0 | 1 |
